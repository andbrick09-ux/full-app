Chapter 01
Login & Role Selection
First-time setup · PIN protection

Each role is protected by its own 4-digit PIN stored on-device. First login walks through PIN creation — subsequent visits just require the correct PIN.

👑
Sir — Dominant
Dom
1
Tap the Dominant card (👑)
On the login screen.
2
Create a 4-digit PIN (first time only)
Enter, then confirm. Stored on-device only, never transmitted.
3
Enter PIN on return visits
App logs in automatically after the 4th digit.
🌸
Sub — Submissive
Sub
1
Tap the Submissive card (🌸)
Your PIN is entirely separate from Sir's.
2
Create & confirm a 4-digit PIN
Stored locally on this device.
3
Daily check-in appears after login
A mood panel slides up automatically if you haven't checked in today. See Chapter 11.
🔐
PIN security
PINs live in device local storage only. Sessions persist within a browser tab but clear when the tab is closed.

Chapter 03
Tasks
Creating · Completing · Filtering

Tasks are the core of the system. Sir assigns them with full control over difficulty, schedule, and linked consequences. The Sub completes them to earn points.

👑
Creating a Task
Dom Only
1
Tap ➕ on the Tasks tab
Opens the New Task sheet from the bottom.
2
Enter title & description
Title shows prominently on the Sub's card. Description holds full instructions.
3
Set difficulty, points & schedule
Difficulty applies a multiplier (Chapter 04). Schedules: One-off, Daily, Weekdays, Weekends, Weekly, Monthly.
4
Optionally: due date, linked punishment, photo proof
Linked punishment auto-triggers on failure. Photo proof requires image submission before points are awarded.
5
Tap "Assign Task"
Appears on the Sub's Tasks tab instantly.
🌸
Completing a Task
Sub View
1
Find the task on the Tasks tab
Overdue tasks are red. Difficulty, schedule, and point value are on each card.
2
Tap "Complete" (or "Submit Proof" for photo tasks)
Points awarded instantly for standard tasks. See Chapter 05 for proof tasks.
3
Toast confirms points & streak
Shows points earned and current consecutive-day streak.
🔍
Filter Button (top-right)
Cycles: All → Pending (incomplete only) → Done (completed today).
Chapter 04
Points & Difficulty
Multipliers · Balance · Earned vs Lost

Every task has a base point value set by Sir. Difficulty applies a multiplier — rewarding harder tasks proportionally. Points are tracked in three visible buckets at the top of the Tasks tab.

DifficultyMultiplier10 base pts →Intended for
Simple×110 ptsQuick everyday habits
Standard×1.515 ptsModerate effort
Demanding×220 ptsSustained effort or vulnerability
Extreme×330 ptsHigh-challenge or significant tasks
⭐
The Points Bar
Always visible at the top of Tasks:

Balance — current spendable total
Earned — cumulative points earned all-time
Lost — cumulative deductions from failures & redeemed rewards
💡
Economy tip
Set base points to reflect effort, then let difficulty do the scaling. A 10pt Extreme task earns 30pts — the difficulty badge signals the challenge level clearly.
Chapter 05
Photo Proof
Submission · Review · Approval

When Sir enables "Require Photo Proof," the Sub cannot self-complete the task. She submits a photo — Sir reviews it in the Queue before points are awarded.

📷
Submitting Proof
Sub View
1
Tap "Submit Proof" on the task card
Tasks needing proof show a blue 📷 badge and this button instead of "Complete."
2
Tap the upload area & choose a photo
Selects from your camera roll. Preview appears immediately.
3
Add an optional note for Sir
Context, time of completion, or anything Sir should know.
4
Tap "Submit for Approval"
Task shows ⏳ Pending. No further action until Sir reviews.
👁
Reviewing Proof
Dom Only
1
📷 Proof card appears in the Queue
Tap "Review Photo" to open the review sheet.
2
View photo & note
Full image shown alongside any note the Sub submitted.
3
Approve or Reject
Approve — awards points, marks complete, updates streak. Reject — clears pending; the Sub resubmits.
Chapter 06
Failures & Consequences
Reporting · Demerit tiers · Consequence Picker

When the Sub cannot complete a task, she reports it honestly. Points are deducted, a demerit is added, and Sir is notified. Repeated failures escalate through three tiers.

🌸
Reporting a Failure
Sub View
1
Tap "Failure" on the task card
The Failure sheet opens.
2
Write a reason — be honest
Sir sees this in the Queue and it's logged in History.
3
Tap "Confess Failure"
Points deducted immediately. Streak resets to zero. Sir notified in Queue.
⚠️
Demerit Tiers
Unresolved failures accumulate. A tier banner appears on the Tasks tab.
Tier 1
1–2 failures · Minor
Dismiss or Redemption available in Queue.
Tier 2
3–4 failures · Moderate
Consequence Picker button unlocks in Queue.
Tier 3
5+ failures · Significant
High-tier consequences available in the picker pool.
⚡
Consequence Picker
Dom Only · Tier 2+
1
Tap "⚡ Consequence" on a failure in Queue
Three consequences drawn randomly from the curated pool.
2
Tap 🎲 Roll Again to refresh
Re-draws three new options from the eligible tier pool.
3
Select one and tap "Apply"
Created as a Redemption task (photo proof required). Failure resolved.
Chapter 06 continued
Redemption Tasks
Custom forgiveness · Point restoration

Alongside the Consequence Picker, Sir can write a fully custom Redemption Task — a specific act the Sub must perform to earn forgiveness and restore points.

🌿
Assigning a Custom Redemption
Dom Only
1
Tap "🌿 Redemption" on a failure in the Queue
Also accessible from the active punishment banner on the Tasks tab.
2
Write title & full instructions
Be specific — exactly what the Sub must do and how it should be documented.
3
Set points restored on completion
What the Sub earns back once Sir approves the proof photo.
4
Tap "Assign Redemption"
A purple 🌿 Redemption task appears in the Sub's list. Completing it resolves the original failure.
⚡
Active Punishment Banner
When an unresolved failure with a linked punishment exists, a red banner appears at the top of the Tasks tab. Sir can set a Redemption Task or Dismiss it directly — no need to navigate to the Queue.
🌿
Redemption on the Sub's side
Redemption tasks have a purple border and a 🌿 badge. They always require photo proof and cannot be self-completed. Once Sir approves, the original failure is resolved and points are restored.

Store management · Redemption · Manual assignment
🎁
Managing Rewards
Dom Only
1
Tap ➕ on Rewards tab — enter details
Title, description, point cost, and an emoji icon.
2
Reward appears in the Sub's store immediately
3
Approve or deny in the Queue
Points only deduct on approval. Denial leaves her balance unchanged.
🌸
Redeeming a Reward
Sub View
1
Browse the Rewards tab
Redeem is greyed out if you lack sufficient points.
2
Tap "Redeem" — request sent to Sir
Shows ⏳ Pending. Points not deducted yet.
3
Await Sir's decision
Approved → points deducted, reward granted. Denied → no change.
⚡
Managing Punishments
Dom Only
1
Tap ➕ on Punishments tab
Enter title, description, point deduction, and emoji.
2
Link to a task via "Attach Punishment if Failed"
Triggers automatically when the Sub reports failure on that task.
3
Or assign manually via "Assign" on any punishment card
Deducts points immediately and creates a Queue entry.
Chapter 09
The Queue
Sir's action center · All pending items

The Queue is Sir's primary action center — a real-time feed of everything needing a decision, aggregated from across the entire app.

TypeWhat it meansActions
📷 ProofPhoto submitted for a proof taskReview → Approve or Reject
⚠️ FailureTask failure reportedConsequence / Redemption / Dismiss
🎁 RewardReward requested from storeApprove (deducts pts) or Deny
🌸 RequestTask request from the Sub's DashboardAssign as Task or Decline
📋
Items sorted newest first
Updates in real time. Once actioned, items disappear automatically. The red badge shows the combined total of unresolved items plus pending task requests.
🌸
Task Requests section
The Sub's task requests appear as a separate section at the bottom of the Queue. Tap "Review" on any card to open the approval sheet.
