Safety & Safewords
✅
Consent is the foundation. Everything in this app is designed around enthusiastic, ongoing, informed consent between adults. No task, scene, or training should ever be performed without clear mutual agreement.
🛑
Safewords Are Absolute
When a safeword is spoken, everything stops immediately — no exceptions, no negotiation. Establish your safeword system before any session. The traffic light system (Red / Yellow / Green) is recommended.
🟡
Traffic Light System
Green — Continue, everything is good.
Yellow — Slow down, check in, something needs adjusting.
Red — Stop completely. No discussion, no delay.
🔄
Consent is Ongoing
Consent given in a previous session does not carry forward automatically. Check in before each session, especially when trying new things from the menus.
💊
Physical Limits
Training programs include safety information specific to each activity. Sharp pain is always a stop signal. Pressure, stretch, and intensity are normal; pain is not. When in doubt, stop and rest.
🧠
Aftercare
After any intense session, emotional and physical aftercare is important for both partners. This app does not manage aftercare — build it into your own protocol.
🚨
Emergency
In any genuine emergency, call 911 or your local emergency services immediately. No scene, task, or training protocol takes priority over real safety.
Frequently Asked Questions
Where is my data saved? expand_more
All training progress, checklist answers, and settings are saved to your device's local storage. No data is sent to any server unless you explicitly use the Cloud Sync feature in the BDSM Compatibility Portal. Clearing your browser data will erase local progress.
How does cloud sync work? expand_more
The BDSM Compatibility Portal uses a unique code to store and retrieve compatibility data online. Both partners can access the same data from different devices using the same code. This is the only part of the app that communicates with an external service.
Can I reset my training progress? expand_more
Yes. Each training module has a Reset All Progress button at the bottom of the protocol tab. This permanently clears your saved data for that module only and cannot be undone.
Does this app work offline? expand_more
Yes. The app is a Progressive Web App (PWA) with a service worker that caches pages for offline use. After the first visit, most pages will load without an internet connection. Cloud sync requires connectivity.
How do I add this to my home screen? expand_more
iPhone/Safari: Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen".

Android/Chrome: Tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App".

Once installed, the app opens full-screen with no browser chrome — it feels native.
What does "mastered" mean in training? expand_more
Each training module defines mastery differently. In Position Training, mastery requires a minimum number of practice days, a photo, and a video sent to Sir for confirmation. In other modules, completing all tasks in a phase unlocks the "Mark Phase Complete" button. Mastery is self-reported — honesty is core to the protocol.
